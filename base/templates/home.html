U&I TRUST

"The U&I Foundation India is a non-profit organization focused on empowering underprivileged communities through education and social initiatives. Founded with the mission to create positive change, U&I works primarily in urban and rural areas to provide quality education to children from disadvantaged backgrounds. The foundation runs various programs, including after-school tutoring, vocational training, and life skills workshops, to ensure holistic development. Additionally, U&I engages volunteers to mentor and teach, fostering a sense of community and social responsibility."

About Us

U&I is a non-profit organization that is committed to creating long-term, positive social change in education, child and women welfare and mental health. Working with neglected communities in nine cities across India, U&I directly impacts 850 people on a weekly basis. What began as a small group of friends passionate about social change has become an organization which has impacted the lives of hundreds of at-risk individuals and inspired thousands to be the change they want to see.
U&I runs two main initiatives -
U&I Teach: The Teach program impacts underprivileged children through after-school education conducted in 21 Learning Centers across the country.
U&I Care: The Care program focuses on the rehabilitation and care of special-needs individuals and trafficked women in government-run homes in Bangalore.

WHY U&I TEACH?

U&I Teach operates with a goal of running high-quality Learning Centers for underprivileged children. In India today overcrowding in government schools and lack of resources has led to a high dropout rate of 90% by high school. U&I tackles this problem through our free, afterschool Learning Centers where children get access to quality education and individualized mentorship. Our core strengths include:

  • Leveraging the power of trained volunteer teachers to tutor children in Math, English, Science and Social Studies.
  • 21 Learning Centers across nine cities reaching 850 children
  • Providing an incredibly low tutor-student ratio of 1:3.
  • Personalized attention through which learning is maximized.
  • Role models for first-generation learners.
  • Holistic education through our 360 Care program which integrates career days, personality development training, counseling sessions, and field trips, as well as setting up libraries.

Our Services

Empowering life through education, U&i foundation builds brighter future for underprivileged children.

TUITIONS PROGRAM

The main focus of the programme is to help our students achieve satisfactory scores in their school examinations, so they clear their grades and move up the ladder. Tuition centers focus on ensuring that the learning lags of students are identified and strategies are employed to help them build the foundational skills they are lacking. The subjects taught are Mathematics, Science, Social Science, Commerce and Economics.

ENGLISH PROGRAM

The English program is tailored to the child's learning level based on a pre-assessment that evaluates their listening, speaking, reading, writing and grammar. There are two main levels- Pre Primary and Primary.

HOLISTIC DEVELOPMENT

Seeks to guide students in developing essential life skills through a 3-6 hour programme implemented in tandem with their academic classes. Our learning centers choose from a set of 9 programmes, according to what would be most suited to the skill lag of students

COUNSELING

Support students to grow holistically and be well-rounded individuals. We partner with professional counsellors to deliver one-on-one or group sessions to our children.

LIFE-INFLUENCING FUNCTIONAL TRAINING (LIFT)

Lift works towards the transition readiness of young adults for when they leave their shelter homes at the age of 18. The students enter the LIFT program at 15 years of age and begin learning valuable, practical life skills to equip them for the real world.

READING PROGRAM

Weekly guided reading sessions are conducted at the centers with the help of library books.
